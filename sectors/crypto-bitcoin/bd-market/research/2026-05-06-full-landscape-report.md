## Verified Market Data (2024–2025)

### Market Sizing
- P2P crypto trading volume: **$1–2B USD** estimated annually
- Bangladesh ranked in **top 30** globally for crypto adoption (Chainalysis)
- Primary trading pair: BDT ↔ USDT on Binance P2P
- Active traders estimated at 500K–1M individuals

### Growth Drivers
- Large unbanked/underbanked population sees crypto as financial access
- Remittance market ($22B+ annually) — crypto as alternative
- Tech-savvy youth demographic (18–35) driving adoption
- USDT/USDC as USD-pegged savings in inflation-protected assets

### Regulatory Notes
- **Trading is legal** — Bangladesh Bank has NOT banned crypto
- Advisory cautions exist but carry no enforcement mechanism
- No crypto exchange licensing framework yet (opportunity for regulation)
- CBDC exploration by Bangladesh Bank (potential future integration)

*Sources: Chainalysis 2024, Bangladesh Bank, local media*
