## Strengths

1. **BD-localized platform** — Remitano.com/bd is the only major exchange with a dedicated Bangladeshi portal. Bengali interface, BD-specific content, and localized support.
2. **bKash + bank transfer support** — Native BDT on/off ramp via bKash and local bank transfers. No need for USDT middle-step like Bybit or KuCoin.
3. **10+ year track record** — Founded 2014. Longest-operating platform serving BD users. Built trust where newer platforms lack history.
4. **Built-in escrow** — P2P escrow system protects both buyers and sellers. Reduces scam risk compared to Telegram-based OTC trading.
5. **Zero-trading-fee promotions** — Remitano frequently runs zero-fee campaigns for BD users, undercutting Binance and Bybit on cost for P2P trades.

## Weaknesses

1. **Low liquidity** — BD P2P market has thin order books. Wide spreads (3-8% above spot) compared to Binance (1-3%). Slower trade execution.
2. **2023 security breach** — Remitano suffered a $2.7M hack in 2023. While funds were restored, the event damaged trust among BD users.
3. **Limited coin selection** — Primarily BTC, USDT, ETH. No altcoins, no futures, no DeFi. BD users wanting more must use other platforms anyway.
4. **No mobile app** — Remitano has no mobile app specifically tailored for BD users. Mobile web experience is inferior to Binance/Bybit apps.
5. **Declining relevance** — As Binance P2P grew BDT support and competitors like Bybit/OKX added P2P, Remitano's unique BD advantage has eroded. BD market share has been declining since 2023.

---

## Threat Level: Medium

Remitano was the first BD-focused exchange but has been losing ground to Binance and Bybit. Its localized BD portal is still a differentiator, but thin liquidity and a 2023 hack make it a secondary choice for most BD users. A new BD crypto platform could absorb its remaining user base by offering better liquidity and product breadth.
