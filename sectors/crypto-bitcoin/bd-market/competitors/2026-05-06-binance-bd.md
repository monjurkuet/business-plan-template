## Strengths

Largest global exchange with significant BD user base. P2P marketplace supports BDT via bKash/bank transfer. Deep liquidity. Mobile app is best-in-class.

## Weaknesses

No official BD presence. Regulatory risk — Bangladesh Bank could block access. P2P scams are common. No legal recourse for users.

---

## Strategy Observation

P2P marketplace that serves BD users despite regulatory prohibition. Network effects make it hard to displace even without official presence.
