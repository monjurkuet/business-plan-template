# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Crypto Business

**Date:** 2026-05-06

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 0-3) — Education First

**Goal:** Build audience and trust through crypto education

- Launch YouTube channel (Bengali) covering: crypto basics, BD regulatory updates, stablecoin for freelancers
- Create Telegram community (target: 5K members in 3 months)
- Publish weekly market analysis + BD-specific insights
- Free introductory workshops (online, twice monthly)
- Revenue: YouTube ads + donations (minimal)

**KPIs:** 5K TG members, 10K YT subs, 100+ workshop attendees

---

## Phase 2: Revenue (Months 3-6) — Monetize the Audience

**Goal:** Convert audience into paying customers

- Launch paid crypto course ($20-50/person) — BD's first structured certification
- Start crypto advisory service for HNWI ($200-500/session)
- Partner with local fintech for compliant stablecoin remittance MVP
- Organize in-person crypto meetup in Dhaka (monthly)
- Revenue: Course sales + advisory fees (target: $2K/month)

**KPIs:** 500+ course enrollments, 20+ advisory clients, $2K MRR

---

## Phase 3: Scale (Months 6-12) — Build the Platform

**Goal:** Create the go-to BD crypto ecosystem

- Build web platform with: courses, advisory booking, community forums, BD market data
- Launch stablecoin remittance product (partnership with existing P2P + fintech)
- Enterprise blockchain consulting for BD businesses
- Apply for relevant licenses/registrations as regulatory framework develops
- Revenue: Platform subscriptions + remittance fees + consulting (target: $10K/month)

**KPIs:** 20K+ platform users, $10K MRR, 5+ enterprise clients

---

## Phase 4: Dominate (Months 12-24) — Market Leadership

**Goal:** Become the #1 BD crypto brand

- Expand to Chittagong, Sylhet, other cities (in-person presence)
- Launch crypto index fund / managed portfolio (if regulations permit)
- Build BD's first regulated crypto-to-fiat on/off ramp
- International partnerships (exchanges, DeFi protocols)
- Revenue: Diversified ($50K+/month)

**KPIs:** 100K+ community, $50K+ MRR, regulatory licenses secured
