# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Crypto (Legal Opportunities Only)

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

⚠️ This plan focuses exclusively on LEGAL opportunities: education, blockchain development, and content creation.

---

## Launch Strategy

**Soft launch:** Week 1 — Bengali crypto education YouTube channel + Facebook page
**Full launch:** Month 2 — Premium course launch + blockchain dev team formation

---

## Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Education + Content)

**Goal:** 10K YouTube subscribers, 5K FB followers, first premium course sold

- **YouTube channel:** 3 videos/week (crypto basics, market analysis, blockchain explained in Bengali)
- **Facebook page:** Daily posts, Reels, market updates
- **Premium course:** "Blockchain & Crypto Fundamentals" — 2,000-5,000 BDT per enrollment
- **Community:** Free Telegram group for Q&A and discussion

## Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Blockchain Dev Services)

- Train 3-5 BD developers in Solidity/Rust (8-week program)
- Launch blockchain dev service for international clients
- Upwork/Fiverr + direct outreach to Web3 projects
- Target: 2-3 international clients, $5K-10K/month revenue

## Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Scale + Positioning)

- Expand dev team to 10+ developers
- Launch "Crypto Academy BD" as branded education platform
- Position as BD's most credible crypto/blockchain resource
- If regulations change: be ready to launch compliant trading/remittance product
- Target: 50K+ YouTube subscribers, 10+ dev clients, 15-20 lakh BDT/month
