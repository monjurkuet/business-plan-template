    11|## Legal Status
    12|
    13|**Crypto trading is LEGAL in Bangladesh.** Bangladesh Bank has issued advisory cautions about the risks of cryptocurrency but has NOT prohibited trading. Some banks may restrict crypto-linked transactions at their discretion, but there is no legal prohibition on buying, selling, or holding cryptocurrency.
