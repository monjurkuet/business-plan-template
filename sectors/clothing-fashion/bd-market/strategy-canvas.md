## ERRC Grid (Eliminate–Reduce–Raise–Create)

### Eliminate
- Physical stores (100% online, at least initially)
- Heritage positioning (we're new, not legacy)
- Slow seasonal collections (move to weekly drops)

### Reduce
- Price (keep accessible but not cheapest)
- Over-design (clean, modern, wearable — not costume-y)

### Raise
- Modest fashion depth (10x what anyone else offers)
- Size inclusivity (XS to 5XL)
- Social media content output (daily Reels, not weekly posts)
- Trend speed (design → sell in 2 weeks, not 3 months)

### Create
- Modest + trendy intersection (currently no one does both well)
- Community-driven design (customers vote on next drop)
- WhatsApp-first shopping experience (BD's #1 messaging app)

---

## Value Proposition

**"Trendy modest fashion for every body."**

The intersection of modest, trendy, and size-inclusive. No one in Bangladesh occupies this position. Aarong is modest but not trendy. Ecstasy is trendy but not modest. Sailor is affordable but neither modest nor inclusive.
