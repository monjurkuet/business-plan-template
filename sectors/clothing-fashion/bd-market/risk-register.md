# Risk Register — Bangladesh Clothing & Fashion

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Risk Assessment

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Score | Mitigation | Status |
|---|------|-----------|--------|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Low demand / no product-market fit | Medium | High | 🔴 High | Pre-order model validates demand before production. Start small, test fast. | Monitor |
| 2 | Quality inconsistency | High | Medium | 🔴 High | Use 2-3 vetted factories. Quality check every batch. Replace underperforming suppliers fast. | Active |
| 3 | Delivery delays | Medium | Medium | 🟡 Medium | Partner with reliable couriers (Pathao/Steadfast). Buffer 24h in promised delivery times. | Monitor |
| 4 | Copycat competitors | High | Low | 🟡 Medium | Community + brand = moat. Products can be copied; relationships can't. Speed of drops > protection of designs. | Accept |
| 5 | Cash flow pressure | Medium | High | 🔴 High | Pre-order model = cash upfront. No inventory build-up. Negotiate 30-day payment with suppliers. | Active |
| 6 | FB/IG account suspension | Low | High | 🟡 Medium | Diversify to WhatsApp + website early. Don't violate platform policies. Keep backup contact lists. | Monitor |
| 7 | Return fraud | Medium | Low | 🟢 Low | Clear return policy (7 days, unused, tags on). Video proof for high-value returns. | Accept |
| 8 | Seasonal demand fluctuation | High | Medium | 🟡 Medium | Eid/wedding seasons drive 40%+ of annual revenue — plan inventory accordingly. Off-season focus on basics. | Active |

---

## Key Assumptions to Validate

1. **Modest fashion demand is real and underserved** — Test with first 100 orders
2. **Customers will buy online without trying on** — Track return rate closely
3. **Size-inclusive positioning resonates** — Monitor which sizes sell first
4. **Pre-order model works for fashion** — Cash collection vs. delivery rate
5. **Instagram is the right primary channel** — Compare conversion rates across channels

---

## Kill Criteria

- **<30 orders in first 60 days** → Pivot or abandon
- **Return rate >15% consistently** → Quality issue, pause and fix
- **CAC > 2x AOV after 90 days** → Unit economics don't work
- **Zero repeat customers after 100 orders** → Product/market fit not achieved
