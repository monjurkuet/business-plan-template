## Government Policy & Regulation

- VAT on apparel retail: **5–15%** slab (National Budget 2024–25)
- Import duty on raw cotton: **5%** (reduced from previous)
- Cash incentives for garment exports
- BGMEA policy framework supports export-oriented manufacturing
- National Budget 2024–25: no new major duties on domestic retail

## Top Players (Domestic Retail)

1. **Aarong (BRAC)** — ~$250–300M revenue, 80+ outlets, largest domestic fashion retailer
2. **Cats Eye** — ~$40–60M revenue, premium menswear, 40+ outlets
3. **Yellow (Beximco)** — ~$30–45M revenue, fashion retail chain by Beximco Textiles

## Key Trends

- Fast fashion gaining traction among urban youth (18–35 demographic)
- E-commerce fashion growing 25%+ YoY (Daraz, Aarong online, F-cube)
- Sustainable/eco-fashion niche emerging but small
- Men's formalwear still dominates domestic spend
- Social media (Facebook/Instagram) primary discovery channel
