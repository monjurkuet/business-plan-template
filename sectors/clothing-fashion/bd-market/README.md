## Verified Market Data (DDGS Deep Search, 2024–2026)

- **Aarong**: 29 outlets (2024), 2 new in 2025, BRAC-owned, 320K+ people impacted, 700 small entrepreneurs, Ayesha Abed Foundation
- **Ecstasy**: First store 2009 at Bashundhara City (12K sqft), flagships in Banani (2015) and Dhanmondi (2016)
