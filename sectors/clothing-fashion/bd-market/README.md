# Clothing & Fashion Sector — Bangladesh Market

## Overview

Bangladesh's domestic apparel market is worth $12-15B (2024 est.) with 8-10% CAGR growth. While the country is globally known as a garment manufacturing powerhouse, the domestic retail fashion market is equally vibrant and rapidly digitizing.

**E-commerce penetration:** ~$1B (7-8% of total), growing 15-20% YoY.

**Key segments:**
- Casual/youth fashion (largest, fastest-growing)
- Modest fashion / hijab wear (underserved, massive demand)
- Streetwear / urban (Ecstasy pioneered, many followers)
- Boutique / heritage (Aarong dominates)
- Custom tailoring (digitization opportunity)

**Why this sector:**
- Every Bangladeshi buys clothes — universal market
- Social media commerce is the default discovery channel
- Low entry cost for D2C brands (Instagram + F-commerce)
- Manufacturing base locally available (unlike most other countries)
- Growing middle class = growing aspirational demand
