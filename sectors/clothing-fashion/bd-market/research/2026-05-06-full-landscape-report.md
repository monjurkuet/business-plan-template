    36|    36|    36|## Key Trends
    37|    37|    37|
    38|    38|    38|1. **D2C explosion via Instagram/Facebook** — New brands launch weekly with zero physical presence. The barrier to entry is a phone, a supplier, and a Facebook page.
    39|    39|    39|
    40|    40|    40|2. **Modest fashion is massively underserved** — Bangladesh is a majority-Muslim country, yet few brands do modest fashion well beyond basic hijab lines. There's a gap for curated, trendy modest wear.
    41|    41|    41|
    42|    42|    42|3. **Print-on-demand emerging** — Zero inventory model: customer orders → print → ship. Currently small but growing as local print partners emerge.
    43|    43|    43|
    44|    44|    44|4. **Live commerce via Reels** — Facebook/Instagram Reels are becoming the primary product discovery channel. Brands that master live selling outperform static posters.
    45|    45|    45|
    46|    46|    46|5. **Sustainable fashion niche** — Small but growing among affluent Gen-Z. Refashion, secondhand, and eco-friendly materials are gaining interest.
    47|    47|    47|
    48|    48|    48|6. **Custom tailoring digitization** — 3D body scan apps and online measurement services are emerging. The traditional "tailor moshai" market is ripe for disruption.
    49|    49|    49|
    50|    50|    50|7. **Size inclusivity gap** — Most brands offer S-XL only. Plus-size market (estimated 30%+ of women) is almost entirely unserved.
