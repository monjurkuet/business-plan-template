## Strengths

Pioneer BD streetwear brand. Strong brand identity and youth cult following. Good online sales and social media presence. First-mover in premium urban fashion.

## Weaknesses

Perceived price inflation — paying for brand, not quality. Limited size range (excludes plus-size). Occasional quality issues with prints/fabric. Increasing competition from smaller streetwear brands.
