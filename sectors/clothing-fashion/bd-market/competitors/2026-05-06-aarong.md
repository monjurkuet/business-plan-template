## Strengths

Strongest brand in BD fashion. BRAC-backed with nationwide stores + e-commerce. Artisan/heritage positioning with massive supply chain. Established trust and quality perception.

## Weaknesses

Premium pricing limits mass market reach. Slow to adopt trends — seasonal collections lag behind fast-fashion cycles. Younger audience (18-25) drifting to faster, cheaper brands. E-commerce UX is dated compared to D2C competitors.

---

## Strategy Observation

Heritage + quality + trust. Dominates the gift and occasion-wear market. Less relevant for everyday youth fashion.
