# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Clothing & Fashion

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Launch Strategy

**Soft launch:** Week 1 — Instagram page + WhatsApp ordering + first 3 designs
**Full launch:** Month 2 — Full collection drop (15-20 SKUs), Facebook Shop, influencer seeding

---

## Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Proof of Concept)

**Goal:** 500 orders, 5,000 Instagram followers, positive unit economics

### Channels
- Instagram (primary) — daily Reels, stories, behind-the-scenes
- WhatsApp Business — order management, customer service
- Facebook Page — support channel, longer-form content

### Content Strategy
- 3 Reels per day (product showcases, styling tips, customer UGC)
- 2 Stories per day (polls, behind-the-scenes, flash sales)
- 1 Live session per week (try-on haul, Q&A, drop announcement)

### First Collection
- 15-20 SKUs: 5 hijab-friendly tops, 3 modest dresses, 3 abaya-adjacent pieces, 4 bottoms, 2-3 accessories
- XS to 5XL size range (major differentiator)
- Price: 800-2,500 BDT range (accessible premium)

### Influencer Seeding
- Gift 20 micro-influencers (5K-50K followers) in modest fashion niche
- Target: 10+ organic posts featuring our products

---

## Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Scale)

**Goal:** 2,000 orders/month, 20K Instagram followers, monthly recurring revenue

### New Channels
- Facebook Ads (conversion campaigns, ₹500-1,000/day budget)
- TikTok Bangladesh (if available)
- Collab with 2-3 established modest fashion influencers (paid partnerships)

### Product Expansion
- Monthly drops (10-15 new SKUs per month)
- Introduce men's modest wear (underserved segment)
- Limited edition collaborations with local artists/designers

### Operations
- Hire 1-2 dedicated packers
- Partner with Pathao/Steadfast for delivery
- Set up bKash/Nagad payment integration

---

## Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Brand Building)

**Goal:** 5,000+ orders/month, 50K+ followers, explore physical presence

### New Channels
- Consider pop-up store in Dhaka (Gulshan/Banani)
- YouTube channel (modest fashion tutorials, styling guides)
- Email/SMS marketing for repeat customers

### Product Expansion
- Occasion wear (Eid, wedding season collections)
- Kids modest wear
- Home textiles (modest = lifestyle, not just clothing)

### Revenue Target
- Month 12: 15-20 lakh BDT/month revenue
- Net margin target: 25-30% after all costs

## Metrics to Track

- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Target <300 BDT
- **AOV (Average Order Value):** Target 1,500-2,000 BDT
- **Repeat Rate:** Target 30%+ within 90 days
- **Fulfillment Time:** Target <48 hours from order to ship
- **Return Rate:** Target <5%
