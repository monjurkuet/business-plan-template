## ERRC Grid (Cross-Niche Strategy)

### Eliminate
- "Build everything from scratch" mindset — use platforms (Foodpanda, Upwork, YouTube) as distribution
- Capital-heavy physical retail — go online-first for every niche

### Reduce
- Time spent on brand building before revenue — sell first, brand later
- Product catalog breadth — start with 1 niche product, expand after traction

### Raise
- Content-driven acquisition (every niche benefits from social media content)
- Subscription/recurring models (pet food delivery, retainer clients, course memberships)
- Community building (pet owners, students, freelancers, thrift shoppers all want community)

### Create
- "Niche stack" approach — start with easiest niche, layer on adjacent ones
  - Example: Cloud kitchen → food content → food brand → food product line
  - Example: Freelance agency → agency content → agency training → SaaS tool
- Cross-niche leverage — skills from one niche (content, marketing) transfer to all others

## Recommended Launch Sequence

1. **Month 1-3: Freelancer-to-Agency** (fastest revenue, zero product cost)
2. **Month 4-6: Cloud Kitchen** (diversify into physical product, learn operations)
3. **Month 7-12: EdTech or Smart Home** (based on which aligns with your skills and audience)
