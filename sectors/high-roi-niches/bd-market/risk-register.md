## Kill Criteria by Niche

**Agency:**
- <3 clients after 90 days → Positioning/outreach problem
- Client retention <50% after 6 months → Service quality issue
- Net margin <25% → Pricing or cost problem

**Cloud Kitchen:**
- <30 orders/day after 60 days → Menu or cuisine choice problem
- Food rating <4.0 on Foodpanda → Quality issue, pause and fix
- Food cost >45% of revenue → Pricing or waste problem

**EdTech:**
- <10 students in first cohort → Course/market fit problem
- Completion rate <40% → Content or format problem
- Referral rate <10% → Quality problem

**Smart Home:**
- Product return rate >10% → Quality problem, pause category
- <50 orders in first 60 days → Marketing or product selection issue
