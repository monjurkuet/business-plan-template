# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh High-ROI Niche Markets

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Recommended Strategy: Sequential Niche Launch

Start with the fastest-revenue niche (Freelancer-to-Agency), then diversify. Each niche funds the next.

---

## Phase 1: Months 1-3 — Freelancer-to-Agency

**Goal:** 3-5 retainer clients, 3-5 lakh BDT/month revenue

**Week 1:**
- Define service offering (e.g., social media management, video editing, web dev)
- Create portfolio website + social media profiles
- Send 50 cold outreach messages on Upwork/Fiverr + LinkedIn

**Week 2-4:**
- Land first 1-2 clients at competitive rates
- Deliver exceptional results → get referrals
- Hire 1-2 freelancers for overflow work (arbitrage begins)

**Month 2-3:**
- Build to 3-5 recurring retainer clients
- Systematize delivery (SOPs, templates, quality checklist)
- Monthly revenue target: 3-5 lakh BDT at 40-60% margin

---

## Phase 2: Months 4-6 — Cloud Kitchen (Parallel)

**Goal:** One cloud kitchen cuisine live on Foodpanda, 1-2 lakh BDT/month additional revenue

**Week 1-2:**
- Research cuisine niche (Biryani, Korean, Desserts — pick one with high demand + low competition on Foodpanda)
- Rent small kitchen space (15-20K BDT/month in Dhaka)
- Register with Foodpanda + Pathao Food

**Week 3-4:**
- Test menu with 5-8 items
- Food photography + social media content (your agency skills help here)
- Launch with promotion (50% off first order, etc.)

**Month 5-6:**
- Optimize menu based on order data (kill low-sellers, double down on hits)
- Add direct-order channel (WhatsApp + Facebook page) to reduce platform commission
- Monthly revenue target: 2-5 lakh BDT at 20-35% margin

---

## Phase 3: Months 7-12 — Expand Based on Traction

**Option A: EdTech Micro-Niche** (if you have teaching skill + audience)
- Launch cohort-based course in your expertise area
- 4-week live cohort, 20-30 students, 3,000-5,000 BDT per seat
- Monthly cohort = 60,000-150,000 BDT revenue with 70-90% margin

**Option B: Smart Home Products** (if you have e-commerce + sourcing skill)
- Source 20-30 smart home SKUs from Shenzhen
- Launch on Facebook + website
- Installation service as premium upsell
- Monthly revenue target: 2-5 lakh BDT at 40-60% margin
