# High-ROI Niches — Bangladesh Market

## Market Overview

This sector captures high-growth, high-margin niche opportunities in Bangladesh.

| Niche | Est. Market Size | CAGR | Source |
|-------|-----------------|------|--------|
| EdTech | ~$300–400M | 15–18% | LightCastle; HolonIQ |
| Pet Care | ~$50–80M | 20–25% | Industry estimates |
| Boutique E-commerce | ~$500M–1B | 15–20% | e-CAB estimates |
| Specialty Food & Beverage | ~$200–300M | 12–15% | LightCastle |

## Government Policy & Regulation

- **VAT exemption** on online educational services
- **a2i (Access to Information)** program digital learning initiatives
- **NCTB digital curriculum** mandate
- Pet import regulations (quarantine, documentation)
- Food safety regulations under Bangladesh Food Safety Authority

## Top Players (EdTech)

1. **10 Minute School** — ~$8–12M revenue, 20M+ users, raised $2M+
2. **Shikho** — ~$3–5M revenue, focused on K-12, raised seed funding
3. **Kagoj Kormo** — ~$2–3M revenue, skill development platform

## Key Trends

- EdTech post-COVID adoption sustaining at higher levels
- Pet ownership growing among urban middle class
- F-commerce (Facebook commerce) enabling niche businesses
- Health & wellness niche growing (organic, supplements)
- Creator economy enabling micro-entrepreneurship
