# High-ROI Niches — Bangladesh Market

## Overview

This sector directory covers additional business opportunities in Bangladesh that don't fit neatly into the primary sectors but offer **exceptional effort-to-return ratios**. These are characterized by low capital requirements, fast time-to-revenue, and strong growth trajectories.

Each niche below is a standalone opportunity — pick one or combine several for a diversified portfolio.
