## Verified Market Data (2024–2025)

### EdTech
- Market size: **$300–400M** (LightCastle + BASIS)
- CAGR: **15–18%** (LightCastle 16.5%; HolonIQ ~17% South Asia)
- 10 Minute School: largest platform, 4.9M Facebook followers, 20M+ users
- Shikho: K-12 focus, growing rapidly

### Pet Care
- Emerging market: **$50–80M** estimated
- Growing 20–25% as urban middle class adopts pets
- Pet Club BD: early mover, small but growing

### Boutique E-commerce
- F-commerce (Facebook-based) estimated at **$500M–1B**
- Thousands of small sellers on Facebook groups/pages
- Niche opportunities: artisan crafts, custom clothing, specialty foods

*Sources: LightCastle 2023, BASIS 2024, HolonIQ 2024, The Daily Star 2024*
