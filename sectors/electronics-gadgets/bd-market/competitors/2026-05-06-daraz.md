## Strengths

Alibaba-backed, largest BD e-commerce platform. Massive electronics selection with flash sales and EMI options. Brand trust from scale. Seller ecosystem is large.

## Weaknesses

Fake product issues plague the platform. Delivery delays in tier-2/3 cities. Customer service is outsourced and frustrating. Seller quality inconsistency — buyers don't trust 'Daraz quality'.

---

## Strategy Observation

Marketplace scale play. Win on selection and price. Trust is the weak point they can't easily fix.
