## ERRC Grid

### Eliminate
- Marketplace model (Daraz owns this, don't compete)
- Grey market without warranty (trust gap is the opportunity, not the strategy)

### Reduce
- Physical store footprint (start online + delivery)
- Product catalog breadth (curate, don't carry everything)

### Raise
- Refurbished phone quality + warranty (no one does this well in BD)
- Content quality (reviews, unboxing, comparisons that drive purchase decisions)
- Accessories brand perception (premium packaging, branded, not generic)

### Create
- "Certified Refurbished" iPhone program (6-month warranty, quality grading, buyback guarantee)
- Tech review → commerce funnel (content builds trust → drives own product sales)
- Tech rental marketplace (rent cameras, drones, laptops — event/project demand)
