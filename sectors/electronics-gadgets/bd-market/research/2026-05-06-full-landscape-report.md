## Key Trends

1. **iPhone grey market is massive** — 60%+ of iPhones in Bangladesh are grey imports (Japan, US, UAE). Price savings of 15-25% vs authorized channel. This is a $500M+ informal market.

2. **EMI/buy-now-pay-later driving sales** — Bank EMI partnerships and bKah/Nagad installment options are making premium electronics accessible to middle-class buyers.

3. **Apple ecosystem growing among affluent youth** — iPhone = status symbol in BD. AirPods, Apple Watch, and MacBook demand follows iPhone adoption. Ecosystem lock-in is real.

4. **TWS/audio accessories exploding** — True wireless earbuds market growing 30%+ annually. Budget segment (500-2,000 BDT) is massive volume. Premium segment (AirPods Pro, Sony) has high margins.

5. **Smart home/IoT products emerging** — Smart cameras, robot vacuums, smart lights are entering the BD market. Currently served by random Facebook sellers with no trusted brand.

6. **Used/refurbished phone market is huge but unorganized** — No trusted refurbished phone brand exists in BD. Buying a used phone is stressful (fear of stolen/fake/refurbished-sold-as-new). Trust solution = massive opportunity.

7. **Tech review content → affiliate monetization** — YouTube/Facebook tech reviewers drive purchase decisions. Affiliate commissions from Daraz/Pickaboo are underexploited.

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Start with premium accessories brand** — Source cases, cables, chargers from Shenzhen. Brand for BD market. 60-80% margins. Low risk.
2. **Build tech review content channel** — YouTube/FB reviews drive affiliate revenue AND build trust for your own products.
3. **Launch refurbished iPhone service** — Buy used from Japan/US → refurbish → sell with 6-month warranty. Trust + quality = brand moat.
4. **Add tech rental** — Cameras, drones, laptops for events/projects. High demand, low competition.
