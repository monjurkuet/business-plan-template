    37|## Key Trends
    38|
    39|1. **iPhone grey market is massive** — 60%+ of iPhones in Bangladesh are grey imports (Japan, US, UAE). Price savings of 15-25% vs authorized channel. This is a $500M+ informal market.
    40|
    41|2. **EMI/buy-now-pay-later driving sales** — Bank EMI partnerships and bKah/Nagad installment options are making premium electronics accessible to middle-class buyers.
    42|
    43|3. **Apple ecosystem growing among affluent youth** — iPhone = status symbol in BD. AirPods, Apple Watch, and MacBook demand follows iPhone adoption. Ecosystem lock-in is real.
    44|
    45|4. **TWS/audio accessories exploding** — True wireless earbuds market growing 30%+ annually. Budget segment (500-2,000 BDT) is massive volume. Premium segment (AirPods Pro, Sony) has high margins.
    46|
    47|5. **Smart home/IoT products emerging** — Smart cameras, robot vacuums, smart lights are entering the BD market. Currently served by random Facebook sellers with no trusted brand.
    48|
    49|6. **Used/refurbished phone market is huge but unorganized** — No trusted refurbished phone brand exists in BD. Buying a used phone is stressful (fear of stolen/fake/refurbished-sold-as-new). Trust solution = massive opportunity.
    50|
    51|7. **Tech review content → affiliate monetization** — YouTube/Facebook tech reviewers drive purchase decisions. Affiliate commissions from Daraz/Pickaboo are underexploited.

## Verified Market Data (2024–2025)

### Market Sizing
- Total consumer electronics: **$9.2B USD** (Statista 2024)
- Mobile phones: ~$4–5B (largest sub-segment)
- Home appliances: ~$2–3B (AC, fridge, washing machine)
- IT/gadgets: ~$1–2B (laptops, tablets, accessories)

### Growth Drivers
- 130M+ mobile subscribers, smartphone penetration ~55%
- Local assembly incentives reducing device costs
- E-commerce platforms (Daraz, Pickaboo, Star Tech) expanding access
- 5G rollout planned → device upgrade cycle
- Home appliance demand driven by urbanization

*Sources: BTRC 2024, Statista 2024, The Daily Star 2024*
