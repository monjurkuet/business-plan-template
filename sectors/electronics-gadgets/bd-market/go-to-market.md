# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Electronics & Gadgets

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Launch Strategy

**Soft launch:** Week 1 — Premium accessories brand on Instagram/Facebook + tech review YouTube channel
**Full launch:** Month 2 — 50+ accessory SKUs + first 10 certified refurbished iPhones listed

---

## Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Accessories + Content)

**Goal:** 200 accessory orders, 5K YouTube subscribers, first refurbished iPhones sold

- **Accessories brand:** Cases, cables, chargers, screen protectors. Source from Shenzhen (Alibaba). Custom packaging with BD branding. Price: 300-1,500 BDT range.
- **YouTube channel:** 3 videos/week (unboxing, comparisons, "best under X BDT" series). Bengali language.
- **Facebook/Instagram:** Product showcases, deals, tech tips. Daily content.
- **Refurbished iPhones:** Buy 20-30 used units from Japan/US. Refurbish (battery, screen if needed). Grade A/B/C. Sell with 6-month warranty.

## Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Scale + Trust)

- Expand refurbished program to 50+ units/month
- Add Samsung/Xiaomi refurbished options
- Launch tech rental service (cameras, drones, laptops)
- Partner with corporate clients for bulk rentals
- Target: 100+ accessory orders/month, 30+ refurbished phone sales/month, 10+ rentals/month

## Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Brand Building)

- "Certified Refurbished" becomes recognized brand in BD
- Add smart home/IoT products to accessory catalog
- Explore physical experience center in Dhaka (not a store — a demo/play space)
- Corporate buyback program (companies upgrade phones → we buy old ones → refurbish → sell)
- Target: 300+ accessory orders, 80+ refurbished phones, 30+ rentals/month, 10-15 lakh BDT/month revenue
