# Risk Register — Bangladesh Travel & Tourism

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Risk Assessment

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Score | Mitigation | Status |
|---|------|-----------|--------|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Visa rejection for clients | Medium | Medium | 🟡 Medium | Set realistic expectations, thorough document review, interview prep. Offer partial refund on rejection. | Active |
| 2 | Government Hajj policy changes | Low | High | 🟡 Medium | Diversify beyond Hajj — add Umrah, leisure travel. Stay compliant with regulations. | Monitor |
| 3 | Domestic tourism seasonality | High | Medium | 🟡 Medium | Peak: Oct-Mar (winter). Off-season: focus on visa processing and international tours. | Active |
| 4 | OTA price war (flights) | High | Low | 🟢 Low | Don't compete on flights. Compete on service + curation. | Accept |
| 5 | Travel safety incidents | Low | High | 🟡 Medium | Partner with established operators, maintain safety standards, have emergency protocols. Insurance upsell. | Monitor |
| 6 | Negative reviews/reputation damage | Medium | High | 🔴 High | Over-deliver on first 100 customers. Personal follow-up on every trip. Address complaints within 24h. | Active |

---

## Kill Criteria

- **Visa approval rate <60%** → Service quality issue, pause and improve process
- **<20 visa applications in first 60 days** → Demand validation failed
- **Tour cancellation rate >30%** → Quality or demand problem
- **Hajj/Umrah partner relationship breaks down** → Find new partner within 30 days or exit segment
