## Key Trends

1. **Domestic tourism explosion** — Cox's Bazar, Sylhet, and Bandarban are seeing 30%+ year-over-year growth in visitors. New resorts and experiences opening monthly.

2. **Hajj/Umrah is a $2B+ stable market** — 130,000+ Bangladeshis perform Hajj annually. Umrah demand is growing faster. Government-licensed agencies dominate but there's room for sub-agents.

3. **Boutique/glamping experiences** — Luxury tent camping, eco-resorts, and curated homestays are emerging. Premium domestic tourism is an underserved niche.

4. **Travel content creators** — YouTube and Facebook travel vloggers are driving destination discovery. Monetization through affiliate links and brand deals is underexploited.

5. **Visa processing services** — High-margin (50-100% markup per application), low-overhead business. Document preparation + appointment booking for US, UK, Schengen, and Asian visas.

6. **OTA consolidation** — ShareTrip and GoZayaan are burning cash to acquire users. Niche players must avoid head-on competition.

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Start with visa processing** — Lowest capital, highest margin, immediate demand. Build trust and customer base.
2. **Add Hajj/Umrah as sub-agent** — Partner with licensed operator, earn commission per pilgrim. Stable, recurring revenue.
3. **Launch boutique domestic tours** — Curated experiences (not budget buses). Instagram marketing drives bookings.
4. **Build travel content presence** — YouTube/Facebook travel content monetized through OTA affiliate links. Long-term asset.

## Verified Market Data (2024–2025)

## Market Sizing
- Total tourism GDP contribution: **$10.7B** (WTTC 2024)
- Direct market (travel services, hotels, transport): ~$4.5B
- Domestic tourism spend: ~$3B+ (growing rapidly)
- Outbound travel: ~$1.5–2B (significant for BD)

## Growth Drivers
- Middle class expansion → more discretionary travel spend
- Online booking platforms reducing friction
- New hotel developments in Cox's Bazar (5-star properties)
- Government investing in tourism infrastructure
- Domestic travel growing 15%+ YoY

*Sources: WTTC 2024, Bangladesh Parjatan Corporation, The Business Standard 2024*
