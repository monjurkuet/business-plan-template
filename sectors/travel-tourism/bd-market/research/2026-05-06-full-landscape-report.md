    35|    35|## Key Trends
    36|    36|
    37|    37|1. **Domestic tourism explosion** — Cox's Bazar, Sylhet, and Bandarban are seeing 30%+ year-over-year growth in visitors. New resorts and experiences opening monthly.
    38|    38|
    39|    39|2. **Hajj/Umrah is a $2B+ stable market** — 130,000+ Bangladeshis perform Hajj annually. Umrah demand is growing faster. Government-licensed agencies dominate but there's room for sub-agents.
    40|    40|
    41|    41|3. **Boutique/glamping experiences** — Luxury tent camping, eco-resorts, and curated homestays are emerging. Premium domestic tourism is an underserved niche.
    42|    42|
    43|    43|4. **Travel content creators** — YouTube and Facebook travel vloggers are driving destination discovery. Monetization through affiliate links and brand deals is underexploited.
    44|    44|
    45|    45|5. **Visa processing services** — High-margin (50-100% markup per application), low-overhead business. Document preparation + appointment booking for US, UK, Schengen, and Asian visas.
    46|    46|
    47|    47|6. **OTA consolidation** — ShareTrip and GoZayaan are burning cash to acquire users. Niche players must avoid head-on competition.
