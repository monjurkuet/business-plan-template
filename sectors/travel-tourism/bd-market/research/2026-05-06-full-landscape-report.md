## Verified Market Data (2024–2025)

### Market Sizing
- Total tourism GDP contribution: **$10.7B** (WTTC 2024)
- Direct market (travel services, hotels, transport): ~$4.5B
- Domestic tourism spend: ~$3B+ (growing rapidly)
- Outbound travel: ~$1.5–2B (significant for BD)

### Growth Drivers
- Middle class expansion → more discretionary travel spend
- Online booking platforms reducing friction
- New hotel developments in Cox's Bazar (5-star properties)
- Government investing in tourism infrastructure
- Domestic travel growing 15%+ YoY

*Sources: WTTC 2024, Bangladesh Parjatan Corporation, The Business Standard 2024*
