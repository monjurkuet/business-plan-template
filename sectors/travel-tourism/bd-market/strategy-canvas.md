## ERRC Grid

### Eliminate
- Flight booking as core offering (commoditized, margin pressure)
- Budget mass-market positioning (ShareTrip/GoZayaan own this)

### Reduce
- Hotel aggregation (thin margins, heavy ops)
- Price-based competition

### Raise
- Visa processing expertise (no one does this well)
- Domestic tour curation quality (not just booking, but experience design)
- Content-driven customer acquisition (cheaper than paid ads)

### Create
- "Travel concierge" service (end-to-end: visa + flight + hotel + experience)
- Hajj/Umrah with modern UX (traditional service + app convenience)
- Travel content studio (in-house content that drives organic bookings)
