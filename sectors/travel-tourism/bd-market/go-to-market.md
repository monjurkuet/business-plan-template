# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Travel & Tourism

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Launch Strategy

**Soft launch:** Week 1 — Visa processing service for top 5 destinations (US, UK, Schengen, Thailand, Malaysia)
**Full launch:** Month 2 — Add Hajj/Umrah sub-agent partnership, first domestic tour package

---

## Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Visa Processing Focus)

**Goal:** 100 visa applications processed, 5,000 BDT average revenue per application

- Target: Students (US/UK/Canada), business travelers (Schengen), tourists (Thailand/Malaysia)
- Marketing: Facebook groups for expats/students, Google Ads for visa-related keywords
- Service: Document review + form filling + appointment booking + interview prep
- Price: 3,000-8,000 BDT per application (depending on destination)

## Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Hajj/Umrah + Domestic Tours)

- Partner with licensed Hajj/Umrah operator as sub-agent
- Launch first curated domestic tour (Sylhet tea garden weekend, Cox's Bazar beach retreat)
- Build Facebook/YouTube content presence (travel vlogs, visa tips, destination guides)
- Target: 50 visa apps/month + 20 tour bookings/month + 10 Umrah referrals/month

## Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Travel Concierge)

- Launch "Travel Concierge" service (full trip planning for 5,000-10,000 BDT fee)
- Add 3-4 curated domestic tour packages
- Build affiliate revenue through OTA partnerships (ShareTrip/GoZayaan referral links)
- Target: 100+ visa apps/month, 50+ tour bookings/month, 5+ lakh BDT/month revenue
