# Sentiment Analysis — Logistics & Transport

## Customer Satisfaction Themes

| Theme | Signal | Sentiment | Evidence |
|-------|--------|:---------:|----------|
| Delivery speed | Top factor: "same-day" vs "next-day" matters | 🟢 Positive | 90%+ of complaints are about speed |
| Tracking visibility | "Where is my parcel?" most asked question | 🟡 Mixed | Real-time tracking still not standard |
| Damage rate | Fragile items broken = immediate competitor switch | 🔴 Negative | 3–5% damage rate reported by small players |
| Customer support | WhatsApp response speed key differentiator | 🟡 Mixed | Chatbots replacing humans (user frustration) |
| COD handling | Cash-on-delivery payment process friction | 🔴 Negative | Wrong change, delayed settlement with merchants |
| Driver behavior | Friendliness + careful handling matters | 🟢 Positive | Some companies (RedX, Paperfly) invest in driver training |
| Price transparency | "Delivery charge unexpected variable" complaint | 🔴 Negative | Dimensional weight vs actual weight confusion |

## Voice of Customer (Common Complaints)

1. **"Amader parcel 3 din delivery time"** — Delivery taking longer than promised/standard
2. **"Parcel jay kotha janina"** — No tracking visibility, no proactive updates
3. **"Parcel bhenge gese"** — Package arrived damaged
4. **"COD settlement late hoise"** — Delayed COD payment settlement (merchant complaint)
5. **"Driver kintu delivery na diye bole"** — Driver didn't attempt delivery, marked "customer unavailable"
6. **"Eid er age parcel painai"** — Pre-Eid delivery rush not adequately managed

## Improvement Levers

1. **Real-time tracking** (SMS/WhatsApp auto-updates at every scan) → +1.0 rating
2. **Delivery guarantee** (free re-delivery if late) → +0.8 rating
3. **COD settlement within 72 hours** (for merchants) → +1.5 merchant NPS
4. **Damage guarantee** (photo-based claim, refund within 7 days) → +1.0 rating
5. **Pre-Eid capacity planning** (3× staff + temporary warehouses Nov–Dec) → seasonal satisfaction
