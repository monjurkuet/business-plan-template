## Eliminate–Reduce–Raise–Create (ERRC)

### Eliminate
- **Showroom-only dependency** — most sales happen online/WhatsApp, don't overspend on showroom real estate
- **Single-brand lock-in** — don't tie yourself to one oil brand (JDA's trap)

### Reduce
- **Generic brand advertising** — Shell spends on corporate branding; you don't need to
- **Physical locations in early stage** — one service center is enough to start; expand later

### Raise
- **Aftercare guarantees** — 1yr engine + 2yr hybrid battery should be the MINIMUM, not the ceiling
- **Bengali educational content** — Reels, YouTube, blog — become the car care education brand
- **Product range** — car care + oil + hybrid parts + accessories = widest selection
- **Transparency** — auction sheets, pricing, reviews — BD market is opaque, be the transparent one

### Create
- **Car care subscription plan** — ৳999/month for quarterly service, priority access, discounts
- **Facebook community group** — "Car Owners BD" — free advice builds organic reach
- **Bank financing for pre-order + products** — bundle car purchase with product care plan
- **SEO content engine** — own Google search for "best [product] for [car model] in Bangladesh"
- **Service center as experience center** — not just repair, but car care demo, product trial, community events
