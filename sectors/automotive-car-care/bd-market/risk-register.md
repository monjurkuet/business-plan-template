## Kill Criteria

- **If product orders don't reach 30/month by M3** — product-market fit not validated; reassess product selection
- **If CAC exceeds ৳800 and doesn't improve by M4** — content/ads strategy failing; pivot acquisition approach
- **If pre-order car delivery fails 2+ times due to import issues** — regulation/supply chain risk too high; deprioritize car import, focus on products
- **If service center can't cover rent by M6** — wrong location or too early; consider closing or relocating

---

## Contingency Plans

**If primary revenue (product sales) underperforms:**
Pivot to service-first model — use service center revenue to subsidize product development. Service margins are higher.

**If CAC is 2x projected:**
Shift budget from Facebook Ads to organic content. Pause paid ads. Focus on Reels + SEO + WhatsApp community.

**If a well-funded competitor enters the space:**
Double down on community (Facebook Group), speed (same-day delivery inside Dhaka), and personalization (WhatsApp relationship). Big competitors can't match personal service speed.
