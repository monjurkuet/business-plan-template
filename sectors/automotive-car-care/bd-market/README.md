## Verified Market Data (DDGS Deep Search, 2024–2026)
### BRTA Vehicle Statistics (Verified 2024)
- Total registered vehicles: **5.35M+** (2023), projected 5.6–5.8M by 2024
- Private cars: **400,000–450,000**
- New registrations: **~462,000/year** (FY2022-23)
- Car penetration: **8–10 per 1,000 people** (vs 50+ India, 800+ US)
- Dhaka division: **~40% of all vehicles** (~1.2M+)

### Reconditioned Car Market (BRVIDA)
- Annual imports: **40,000–50,000 units/year**
- Market size: **৳8,000–10,000 crore (~$750M–1B)**
- Source: **90% from Japan**
- Toyota dominates: **60%+ market share**
- Government revenue from import duties: **৳15,000+ crore** (FY2022-23)

### Auto Parts & Accessories
- Auto parts imports: **~$500–700M annually** (Bangladesh Bank/NBR)
- Car accessory market: **৳500–800 crore (~$50–80M)**, growing 15–20% annually
- Car care products market: Growing segment (dedicated market report 2025–2031)
- Car detailing services: Exterior & interior segments, organized sector emerging (2024–2030 report)

### Automotive Service Market
- Total service market: **৳2,000–3,000 crore (~$200–300M)**
- Workshops nationwide: **50,000–80,000** (80–85% informal/unorganized)
- Car wash franchise: Emerging — SMERGERS lists franchise opportunities in BD
- Automotive lubricants: Passenger vehicles 48.78% of market (2024)
