## Unit Economics

**Average order value (AOV) by service:**
- Car wash: ৳800 avg
- Full detailing: ৳12,000 avg
- Ceramic coating: ৳40,000 avg
- PPF: ৳75,000 avg

**Weighted average order value (mix-adjusted):** ৳6,000/service

**Blended COGS (avg variable cost):** ৳2,100/service

**Blended gross margin:** ~65%

**Customer acquisition cost (CAC):**
- Facebook/Instagram ads: ৳150–300/lead → ৳300–600/booked service
- Walk-in / organic: ৳0–50
- Referral (offered ৳200 discount): ৳200
- **Blended CAC: ৳350**

**Expected customer lifetime:**
- Car wash: 12 months (recurring)
- Detailing/coating: 24–36 months (repeat or referral)
- **Weighted avg lifetime: 18 months**

**Lifetime value (LTV):**
- Avg monthly spend per active customer: ৳1,200 (1.5 washes + occasional detail)
- Avg lifetime: 18 months
- **LTV: ৳21,600**

**LTV:CAC ratio:**
- 21,600 / 350 ≈ **62:1** (strong; driven by high retention on subscription/repeat wash)

## Key Assumptions

1. **Location premium:** Rent of ৳800K/month assumes a prime Dhaka location (Gulshan/Banani/Bashundhara). A secondary location halves this but reduces foot traffic by 40–50%.
2. **Staff productivity:** Each technician performs 3–5 services/day. Below 2 services/day the model loses money on labor.
3. **Seasonality:** Revenue drops 20–30% in monsoon months (June–August). Offset by higher demand Nov–Feb (post-monsoon + wedding season).
4. **CAC stability:** Assumes Facebook/Instagram ads remain cost-effective at ৳150–300/lead. Rising competition could push CAC to ৳500+ within 12 months.
5. **Service mix:** Model assumes 40% wash, 35% detailing, 15% coating, 10% PPF. A heavier PPF mix improves AOV but slows job throughput.
6. **Inflation & duties:** Import duties on chemicals, PPF film, and equipment assumed at current rates (15–30%). Any increase could compress margins.
7. **No debt financing:** All startup costs assumed equity-funded. Debt service would add ৳100–150K/month interest.
8. **Customer retention:** 50% monthly retention on car wash, 70% annual return rate for detailing. Drop below 40% monthly retention and unit economics break.
9. **VAT:** 15% VAT included in pricing. Model assumes full compliance.
10. **RISKIEST assumption:** Rent-to-revenue ratio. At 33% of revenue in early months, any revenue shortfall makes fixed costs unsustainable.
