# GarirBazar (গাড়ীর বাজার)

## Overview
- **Type**: Online car marketplace
- **Founded**: ~2016
- **HQ**: Dhaka, Bangladesh
- **URL**: garirbazar.com
- **Facebook**: https://www.facebook.com/garirbazar/ (54,130 likes)

## Business Model
- Commission-based vehicle listing platform
- Buy/sell cars, motorbikes, trucks
- Largest Facebook car group in Bangladesh
- Dealership and individual seller listings

## Key Metrics (Verified)
- **Facebook**: 54,130 likes, active engagement
- **Market Position**: Leading online vehicle marketplace in BD
- **Competitors**: Carmudi BD, Bikroy.com (general classifieds)

## Threat Level: MEDIUM
- Established user base but limited to listing model
- Low technology moat
- Potential to expand into vehicle services/inspection
