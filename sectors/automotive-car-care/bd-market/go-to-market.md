# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Car Care Business

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Launch Strategy

**Soft launch:** Month 1 — Facebook Page + e-commerce website + WhatsApp
**Full launch:** Month 3 — Service center opens + Reels content engine running
**Geography:** Start Dhaka (Kakrail/Badda area), expand to Chittagong by M6

## Content Strategy

**Primary platforms:** Facebook (Reels + Page) + YouTube (long-form) + Blog (SEO)

**Content calendar:**

| Day | Content Type | Example |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| Mon | Educational Reel | "Why your Prius loses mileage — 3 reasons" |
| Tue | Product showcase | New product unboxing/demo |
| Wed | Customer story | Video testimonial in Bengali |
| Thu | Car import update | Auction sheet walkthrough, new arrivals |
| Fri | Tip/FAQ | "What's the right oil for your Aqua?" |
| Sat | Community post | Poll, question, discussion |
| Sun | Behind-the-scenes | Workshop, team, process transparency |

**Key messages to reinforce:**
1. "Guaranteed quality or your money back" — trust
2. "From import to aftercare" — completeness
3. "Bengali experts who understand YOUR car" — local expertise

## 90-Day Plan

### Days 1–30: Foundation
- [ ] Register business entity + trade license
- [ ] Set up Facebook Page + Instagram + YouTube
- [ ] Launch e-commerce website (product catalog + pre-order section)
- [ ] List first 20 SKUs on website
- [ ] Create WhatsApp Business profile
- [ ] Post first 10 Reels
- [ ] Set up Google Business Profile (Maps)
- [ ] Write first 5 SEO blog posts
- [ ] Open Facebook community group

### Days 31–60: Traction
- [ ] Hit 1,000 followers on Facebook
- [ ] Start Facebook Ads (৳5K/month budget)
- [ ] Secure first 10 product customers
- [ ] Secure first 3 pre-order inquiries
- [ ] Sign bank/NBFI financing partnership
- [ ] Sign lease for service center location
- [ ] Post 30 Reels (1/day)
- [ ] Write 10 more blog posts
- [ ] First influencer collaboration

### Days 61–90: Scale Signals
- [ ] Service center soft opening
- [ ] Hit 5,000 followers
- [ ] 50+ product orders/month
- [ ] First pre-order car delivered
- [ ] Launch "Care Plan" subscription
- [ ] 20 blog posts indexed by Google
- [ ] First organic Google search visitors
- [ ] Customer satisfaction survey — target 90%+ satisfaction
