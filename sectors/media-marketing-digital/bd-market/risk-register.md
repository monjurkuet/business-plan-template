# Risk Register — Bangladesh Media, Marketing & Digital Agencies

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Risk Assessment

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Score | Mitigation | Status |
|---|------|-----------|--------|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Client churn (low retention) | High | Medium | 🔴 High | Over-deliver first 90 days. Monthly performance reports. Switch to performance pricing to align incentives. | Active |
| 2 | Platform dependency (FB/Google) | Medium | High | 🔴 High | Diversify across platforms. Build owned channels (email, WhatsApp). Stay current on policy changes. | Monitor |
| 3 | AI disruption of services | Medium | Medium | 🟡 Medium | Be the AI-adopter, not the AI-victim. Use AI tools to increase output and reduce costs. | Active |
| 4 | Talent acquisition/retention | High | Medium | 🔴 High | Hire for potential, not experience. Train aggressively. Competitive pay + creative freedom. Freelance backup. | Active |
| 5 | Scope creep on fixed-price projects | High | Low | 🟡 Medium | Clear scope document per client. Change request process. Track hours even on fixed-price. | Accept |
| 6 | Payment delays | High | Medium | 🟡 Medium | 50% upfront, 50% on delivery for projects. Monthly billing with 7-day payment terms. Late fee after 15 days. | Active |

---

## Kill Criteria

- **<3 clients after 90 days** → Positioning or outreach problem
- **Client retention <50% after 6 months** → Service quality issue
- **Net margin <15% after 6 months** → Pricing or cost problem
- **Team burnout (can't take new clients)** → Hire or die
