# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Media, Marketing & Digital Agencies

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Launch Strategy

**Soft launch:** Week 1 — Instagram/Facebook page showcasing Reels content. Direct outreach to 20 e-commerce brands.
**Full launch:** Month 1 — First 3 clients on monthly content packages. Portfolio building.

---

## Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Content Studio)

**Goal:** 5 paying clients, 2-3 lakh BDT/month revenue

- **Offer:** 20 Reels/month + 30 stories/month for 30,000-50,000 BDT/month
- **Target clients:** E-commerce brands on Facebook with 10K-100K followers
- **Content:** Product showcases, trending audio content, customer testimonials, behind-the-scenes
- **Tools:** CapCut, Canva Pro, ChatGPT, Midjourney (for ad visuals)
- **Team:** 1 content creator + 1 editor (can be freelancer initially)

## Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Full-Stack Agency)

- Add Facebook/Google ads management (15,000-30,000 BDT/month per client)
- Introduce performance-based pricing option (5-15% of attributed sales)
- Build influencer network (50+ micro-influencers across fashion, food, tech)
- Target: 10 clients, 5-7 lakh BDT/month revenue

## Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Growth Partner)

- Offer "Growth Partner" package: content + ads + influencer + analytics
- Revenue-share model for 2-3 high-potential brands (bigger upside, less cash flow pressure)
- Hire 2-3 full-time staff (content + ads + account management)
- Target: 15 clients, 10-15 lakh BDT/month, 30%+ net margin
