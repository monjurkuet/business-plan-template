    35|## Key Trends
    36|
    37|1. **Short-form video dominates** — Reels/TikTok is the #1 content format. Brands that don't have Reels strategy are invisible. Agencies that can produce high-volume, quality short-form are winning.
    38|
    39|2. **AI-generated content emerging** — AI tools (image generation, video editing, copy) are cutting production time by 50-70%. Agencies that leverage AI have a cost advantage.
    40|
    41|3. **Influencer marketing replacing traditional ads** — BD brands are shifting 30-40% of ad budgets to influencer collaborations. This is an entire sub-agency model.
    42|
    43|4. **UGC (user-generated content) for brands** — Authentic customer content is outperforming polished studio content. Agencies are building UGC pipelines.
    44|
    45|5. **E-commerce brands need full-stack agencies** — Not just ads, but creative + media buying + fulfillment + analytics. The demand for integrated services is growing.
    46|
    47|6. **Podcast/long-form YouTube emerging for B2B** — B2B marketing in BD is finding podcasts and YouTube thought leadership. Early mover advantage.

## Verified Market Data (2024–2025)

### Market Sizing
- Digital marketing/advertising: **$220–250M** (Statista + BASIS)
- Total ad market: ~$500M with digital at ~50% share
- Digital share grew from 35% (2020) to 50%+ (2024)
- Social media ad spend: ~$100M+ (Facebook dominant)

### Growth Drivers
- 50M+ Facebook users in Bangladesh → social advertising scale
- E-commerce growth driving performance marketing demand
- Brand shift from TV/print to digital (especially video)
- SMEs adopting digital marketing (Google Ads, FB Ads)
- Creator economy/influencer marketing emerging as major channel

*Sources: BASIS 2024, ADAB, Statista 2024, The Daily Star 2024*
