# Strategy Canvas — Construction & Real Estate

## Blue Ocean ERRC Grid

| Eliminate | Reduce |
|-----------|--------|
| Separate trips to multiple shops (one-stop showroom) | Order fulfillment time (from 2–3 days to same-day) |
| Manual paper estimates (digital quoting) | Pricing opacity (published price list) |
| Language barrier in specs (Bengali spec sheets) | Minimum order quantity (no minimum) |
| Contractor-only pricing (transparent retail) | Payment turnaround for suppliers |

| Raise | Create |
|-------|--------|
| Material quality consistency (brand-authorised) | Project material calculator (web tool) |
| Delivery reliability (guaranteed within 24h) | Contractor credit program (milestone-linked) |
| Showroom experience (samples, catalogs, consultation) | WhatsApp-based quick quoting |
| After-sale support (installation guidance) | Interior-decoration + material supply combo |

## Competing Factors

| Factor | Industry Avg | Our Target | Gap |
|--------|:-----------:|:----------:|:---:|
| Delivery speed | 2–3 days | Same/next day | Logistics, fleet |
| Price transparency | Opaque | Published catalog | Market intelligence |
| Showroom experience | Cluttered | Organized by project type | Layout investment |
| Contractor credit | Rare | Milestone-linked | Working capital |
| Product range | Narrow (specialist) | Broad (one-stop) | Supplier network |
