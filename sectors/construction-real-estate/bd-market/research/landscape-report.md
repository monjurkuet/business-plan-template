# Landscape Report — Construction & Real Estate

## Sector Overview

Bangladesh's construction sector contributes ~8–10% to GDP (FY2024–25). Real estate in Dhaka alone is estimated at Tk 50,000–70,000 Cr. Annual housing demand in Dhaka: ~150,000 units vs supply of ~25,000–30,000 units (RAJUK estimates).

## Key Trends

1. **Affordable housing gap:** ~105,000 unit/year unmet demand in <5,000/sq ft segment
2. **Green building:** LEED-certified projects growing (BGBC certification emerging)
3. **Tiles replacing traditional flooring:** Ceramic/porcelain adoption up 15–20% YoY in urban areas
4. **Pre-engineered steel buildings:** Warehouse/commercial construction shifting from RCC
5. **Smart home:** 2–5% adoption in premium flats (automation, IoT devices)
6. **RAJUK DAP (Detailed Area Plan):** Zoning changes affecting land values and development

## Entry Strategy Recommendation

- **Phase 1:** Material supply showroom (rod, cement, tiles, sanitary, paint) targeting small contractors in Mirpur/Uttara belt
- **Phase 2:** Add interior decoration consultation + supply (high-margin, relationship-driven)
- **Phase 3:** Real estate brokerage/co-development with landowner partnerships (highest capital requirement, highest margin)
