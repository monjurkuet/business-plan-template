# Sentiment Analysis — Construction & Real Estate

## Customer Satisfaction Themes

| Theme | Signal | Sentiment | Evidence |
|-------|--------|:---------:|----------|
| Material quality consistency | Top complaint: rod weight variance (BSRM vs local) | 🟡 Mixed | 5–7% weight deviation reported |
| Price stability | Builders frustrated with rod/cement weekly price swings | 🔴 Negative | 15–20% monthly price swings |
| Delivery punctuality | Critical for contractors (idle labor = cost) | 🟢 Positive | Willing to pay 3–5% premium for guaranteed delivery |
| Credit/terms | Small contractors need credit, large suppliers reluctant | 🔴 Negative | Payment cycle 60–90 days, contractors lack working capital |
| Warranty/honesty | "WhatsApp estimate never matches final bill" — trust gap | 🔴 Negative | Common industry issue |
| Flat delivery promises | Developers routinely miss deadlines by 6–18 months | 🔴 Negative | RAJUK + developer delays compound |
| After-sale service (flat) | Waterproofing, plumbing issues emerge 1–2 years post-handover | 🔴 Negative | Structural warranty rare |

## Voice of Customer (Common Complaints)

1. **"Rod er ojon kom"** — Rod/steel weight under-delivered by 5–7% (common across supply chain)
2. **"Promise moto delivery hoy na"** — Delivery timelines routinely broken
3. **"Flat ready hote 2 bochor laglo, promise chhilo 1 bochor"** — Flat handover delays 2× promised timeline
4. **"Barir niche pana jome"** — Waterproofing failures post-handover
5. **"Ek e gele dam bare 200 taka"** — Same item price quoted differently on repeat visits (no price list culture)
