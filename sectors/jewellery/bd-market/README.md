## Government Policy & Regulation

- **BAJUS mandatory hallmarking** regulation 2023
- VAT on jewellery: **5%**
- Import duty on gold: **~10%** (reduced from 15% in Budget 2023–24)
- **BCSIR purity certification** required
- Consumer Rights Protection enforcement strengthening

## Top Players

1. **Aarong Jewellery (BRAC)** — ~$30–40M, artisan jewellery sub-brand
2. **Pabittra** — ~$20–30M, traditional gold jeweller, 15+ outlets in Dhaka
3. **Diamond World** — ~$15–25M, certified diamond & gold retail, 10+ outlets

## Key Trends

- Gold demand peaks during Eid and wedding season (Oct–Feb)
- Hallmarking regulation increasing consumer trust in organized retail
- Lab-grown diamond awareness growing but niche
- Online jewellery sales emerging via Daraz, Aarong online
- Traditional designs (nakshi kantha motifs, filigree) remain popular
