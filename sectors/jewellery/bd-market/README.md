# Jewellery Sector — Bangladesh Market

## Overview

Bangladesh's jewellery market is worth $3-4B (2024 est., formal + informal), growing at 6-8% CAGR. The market is deeply trust-driven — most purchases happen through family jewellers known for generations.

**E-commerce penetration:** <2% — the lowest of any retail sector. This is both the challenge and the opportunity.

**Key segments:**
- Bridal jewellery (60%+ of market by value)
- Gold traditional (dominant metal)
- Fashion/imitation jewellery (fastest-growing, social media driven)
- Silver/contemporary (emerging Gen-Z segment)
- Lab-grown diamonds (very early stage)

**Why this sector:**
- Cultural necessity — weddings, Eid, gifts demand jewellery
- Massive margins on fashion/imitation pieces (70-80%)
- Online penetration is so low that even 5% capture = $150-200M market
- Gold jewellery doubles as investment/savings in Bangladesh
