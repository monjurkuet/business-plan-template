# Go-to-Market Plan — Bangladesh Jewellery

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Launch Strategy

**Soft launch:** Week 1 — Instagram page with 15-20 fashion jewellery designs + WhatsApp ordering
**Full launch:** Month 2 — 50+ SKU catalog, Facebook Shop, influencer seeding

---

## Phase 1: Months 1-3

**Goal:** 300 orders, 3,000 Instagram followers

- Start with imitation/fashion jewellery (no gold trust barrier)
- Source from China/India, rebrand with custom packaging
- Price range: 200-1,500 BDT (impulse purchase territory)
- Daily Reels: styling videos, unboxing, close-up product shots
- Seed 15 micro-influencers with free pieces for content

## Phase 2: Months 4-6

- Expand to silver jewellery line (higher price, higher trust needed)
- Introduce "Try at Home" service for orders >2,000 BDT
- Launch gift curation boxes for Eid and wedding season
- Target: 1,000 orders/month, 15K followers

## Phase 3: Months 7-12

- Evaluate jewellery rental service launch
- Consider lab-grown diamond entry (if global trend holds)
- Explore popup events in Dhaka hotels/malls
- Target: 3,000+ orders/month, 30K+ followers, 8-10 lakh BDT/month revenue
