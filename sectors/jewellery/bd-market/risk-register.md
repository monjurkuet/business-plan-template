# Risk Register — Bangladesh Jewellery

## Date: 2026-05-06

---

## Risk Assessment

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Score | Mitigation | Status |
|---|------|-----------|--------|-------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Low trust for online jewellery | High | High | 🔴 Critical | Start with imitation (no trust barrier), build reviews, video packaging | Active |
| 2 | Product quality inconsistency | High | Medium | 🔴 High | Vet 2-3 suppliers, sample every batch, replace fast | Active |
| 3 | Copycat designs | High | Low | 🟡 Medium | Speed of new designs > protection. Brand > product. | Accept |
| 4 | Gold price crash (if entering gold) | Low | High | 🟡 Medium | Don't hold gold inventory initially. Consignment model. | Monitor |
| 5 | Delivery theft/damage | Medium | Medium | 🟡 Medium | Insured shipping, photo proof at delivery, discrete packaging | Active |
| 6 | Return fraud | Medium | Low | 🟢 Low | Clear policy, video proof, restocking fee on high-value | Monitor |

---

## Kill Criteria

- **<50 orders in first 60 days** → Pivot product line or abandon
- **Return rate >20%** → Quality problem, pause and fix
- **CAC > AOV after 90 days** → Channel economics broken
