# Landscape Report — Education & Training

## Sector Overview

Bangladesh's education sector spans 20M+ students in 180,000+ institutions. Private tutoring and coaching is a 10,000+ Cr informal market. The training and skill development sector is growing at 12–15% YoY, driven by IT, language, and study abroad demand.

## Key Trends

1. **EdTech adoption:** 30%+ of urban households use supplementary online learning
2. **Freelancer skill training:** 650,000+ verified freelancers on global platforms; training demand surging
3. **IELTS migration boom:** 500K+ IELTS tests/year; UK, Canada, Australia destinations dominate
4. **Automated learning platforms:** AI-driven personalized learning (duolingo model, Bangla versions emerging)
5. **Government digital schools:** 20,000+ schools digitized with multimedia classrooms (a2i program)
6. **Corporate upskilling:** RMG, IT, pharma sectors investing in employee training programs
7. **Special needs education:** Growing but still <10% coverage; legislative push for inclusion

## Entry Strategy Recommendation

- **Phase 1:** Language + skill training center (IELTS/Business English + IT skills) in Gulshan/Banani
- **Phase 2:** Online course platform (recorded content + live classes) with subscription model
- **Phase 3:** Corporate training contracts (RMG compliance training, IT upskilling for corporates)
