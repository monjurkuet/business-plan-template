## Key Trends

1. **Health tech:** Telemedicine platforms (Praktice, Maya) growing 30% YoY; 500K+ consults/month
2. **Pharma export boom:** Bangladesh pharma exporting to 150+ countries; $1B export target by 2028
3. **Medical tourism:** Inbound from Nepal, Myanmar, Middle East for cardiac treatment (30% cheaper than India)
4. **Health insurance penetration:** Only 2–3% of population insured but growing 15% YoY
5. **Generic medicine shift:** 80% of prescriptions are generics; brand substitution is common
6. **Digital health records:** Amended EHR system by Ministry of Health rolling out

## Entry Strategy Recommendation

- **Pharmacy:** Multi-branch pharmacy in Dhaka (DGDA-licensed) with online ordering + delivery (1-hour)
- **Diagnostic:** Mid-range diagnostic center near hospital cluster; focus on accuracy + digital reporting
- **Clinic:** Multi-specialty clinic with online appointment, transparent pricing, and NABL-level QA
