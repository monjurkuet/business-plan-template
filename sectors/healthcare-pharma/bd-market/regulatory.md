## Pharmacy Compliance

- Registered pharmacist must be present during business hours
- Schedule A (Narcotics) records to be maintained separately
- Temperature log for cold chain items (vaccines, insulin)
- Prescription records to be kept for 3 years
- Expired drug disposal protocol with DGDA authorization

## Export Compliance (Pharma)

- WHO-GMP certification mandatory for export
- COPP (Certificate of Pharmaceutical Product) from DGDA
- Halal certificate for ME/South Asian markets
- Validation of manufacturing facility
