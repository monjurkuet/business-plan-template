## Voice of Patient (Common Complaints)

1. **"Doctor dekhte 3 ghonta wait korte hoy"** — Excessive wait times for specialist appointments
2. **"Medicine dam onek beshi, same drug cheaper elsewhere"** — Price variation across pharmacies for same drug
3. **"Diagnostic report e validity thakbe ki na?"** — Suspected quality/reliability of diagnostic reports
4. **"Biller moddhe mysterious charges"** — Unexpected charges on hospital/clinical bills
5. **"Pharmacy te doctor prescription chara drug dicche"** — Over-the-counter sales of prescription drugs (common concern)
