# Strategy Canvas — Healthcare & Pharma

## Blue Ocean ERRC Grid

| Eliminate | Reduce |
|-----------|--------|
| Hidden hospital charges (itemized billing) | Wait time for appointments (from 3h to 30 min) |
| Paper prescription (digital/electronic) | Medicine price variation across pharmacies |
| Separate visits for each test (one-stop diagnostic) | Staff-to-patient friction (SM training) |
| Prescription not valid across branches (chain model) | Counterfeit risk (barcode verification) |

| Raise | Create |
|-------|--------|
| Diagnostic accuracy (DGDA/NNABL-level) | Home health checkup package (tele-consult + sample pickup) |
| Cold chain integrity (temp-monitored delivery) | Corporate health card (prepaid medical wallet) |
| Pharmacy online ordering + tracking | Chronic disease subscription (monthly medicine pack) |
| Doctor availability (app + SMS booking) | Health kiosk (weight, BP, sugar self-check) |
