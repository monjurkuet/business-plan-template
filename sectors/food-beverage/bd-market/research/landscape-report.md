# Landscape Report — Food & Beverage

## Key Trends

1. **Cloud kitchen boom:** 300+ cloud kitchens in Dhaka, 18–20% YoY growth
2. **Plant-based / healthy menu:** Growing segment in Dhaka premium (30% menu share increase in 3 years)
3. **Ghost kitchen franchises:** International models entering (Unilever's "Theube" model)
4. **Farm-to-table:** Farm/Restaurant direct sourcing (10–15% cost saving, quality control)
5. **Digital ordering:** 60%+ of urban orders placed via mobile (app, WhatsApp, food aggregator)
6. **Processed snack shift:** 20% growth in packaged snacks in Tier-2/3 cities
7. **Tea culture resurgence:** Specialty tea shops growing (Tea Co., Japan Tea, Chabbis)
