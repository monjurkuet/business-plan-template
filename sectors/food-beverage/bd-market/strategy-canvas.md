# Strategy Canvas — Food & Beverage

## Blue Ocean ERRC Grid

| Eliminate | Reduce |
|-----------|--------|
| Hidden service charges | Delivery time (from 45–60 min to 25 min) |
| Complicated menu (300+ items) | Wait time at busy hours |
| Printed menu lock-in (digital menu + QR) | Price premium during peak |
| Third-party delivery dependency (build own) | Food waste (dynamic portion sizing) |

| Raise | Create |
|-------|--------|
| Ingredient transparency (source, farm) | Meal subscription (weekly boxes) |
| Hygiene visibility (open kitchen) | Theme nights with local/regional cuisine |
| Loyalty program (points = actual discount) | DIY recipe kit delivery (pause-on-demand) |
| Seasonal menu rotation | Catering marketplace for events |
