# Sentiment Analysis — Food & Beverage

## Customer Satisfaction Themes

| Theme | Signal | Sentiment | Evidence |
|-------|--------|:---------:|----------|
| Taste & flavor consistency | Top factor for repeat visits | 🟢 Positive | 70%+ ratings driven by taste |
| Delivery speed (online food) | #1 complaint for online ordering | 🔴 Negative | 40% of 1-star reviews mention late delivery |
| Hygiene standards | Growing concern post-pandemic | 🟡 Mixed | Higher expectations for dine-in; hygiene seals valued |
| Portion size vs price | Portion shrinkage observed while prices rose | 🔴 Negative | Common social media complaint |
| Staff behavior | Friendly staff = higher tips and repeat visits | 🟢 Positive | Top driver of 4–5 star reviews |
| Menu freshness | "Menu same for years" complaint from regulars | 🟡 Mixed | Churn risk if menus don't rotate seasonally |

## Voice of Customer (Common Complaints)

1. **"Delivery time 1 ghonta bole, 2 ghonta lagse"** — Late delivery, inaccurate ETAs
2. **"Quantity komay dise, dam rekhe dise"** — Portion shrinkage without price reduction
3. **"Menu same, kichu change na"** — Menu stagnation for established restaurants
4. **"Ranna quality same thake na"** — Inconsistent taste across visits or branches
5. **"Khabarer modhye hair"** — Hygiene complaints (occasional, but damaging)

## Improvement Levers

1. **15-min delivery guarantee** (or free next order) → +1.0 online segment rating
2. **Menu rotation twice a year** highlighting seasonal ingredients → +0.5 regular rating
3. **Standardized portion sizes with known grammage** (80% of restaurants don't show weight) → +0.5 rating
4. **Open kitchen / hygiene display** in dining area → +1.0 hygiene-conscious segment
