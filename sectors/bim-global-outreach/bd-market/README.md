## Target Markets (Tier 1)

1. **United States** — Largest BIM market globally. 70%+ adoption in AEC industry. Key hubs: New York, Chicago, San Francisco, Houston, Miami.
2. **United Kingdom** — Second-largest BIM market. Government-mandated BIM Level 2 on public projects since 2016. Key hubs: London, Manchester, Birmingham.
3. **Australia** — Strong BIM adoption driven by construction boom. Key hubs: Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane.
4. **Canada** — Growing BIM mandate. Key hubs: Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary.
5. **UAE** — Rapid construction growth, BIM increasingly mandatory for large projects. Key hubs: Dubai, Abu Dhabi.

## Target Markets (Tier 2)

6. **Saudi Arabia** — NEOM and Vision 2030 driving massive construction demand. Key hubs: Riyadh, Jeddah.
7. **Germany** — Steady BIM adoption in European construction. Key hubs: Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich.
8. **Netherlands** — High BIM adoption rate among European countries. Key hubs: Amsterdam, Rotterdam.
9. **Singapore** — Government-mandated BIM on public projects since 2015. Key hubs: Singapore.
10. **Ireland** — Growing AEC sector with BIM-outsourcing demand. Key hubs: Dublin, Cork.

## Core Services (for iNCOMPLEX positioning)

- **Architectural BIM** — BIM modeling, Revit documentation, construction drawings, shop drawings, as-built modeling, BIM coordination
- **MEP & Building Systems** — HVAC drawings & coordination, plumbing design & documentation, fire safety system drawings, electrical drawings & layouts, MEP clash detection
- **Engineering & Construction** — Civil engineering documentation, structural coordination, construction blueprints, quantity takeoff, Navisworks coordination, scan-to-BIM, LOD 300-400 modeling
- **Visualization** — BIM visualization, Enscape/Lumion support, client presentation graphics, architectural rendering

## Key Trends

- **Government BIM mandates** driving adoption in US, UK, Australia, UAE, Singapore, Saudi Arabia
- **Prefabrication + DfMA** requiring higher LOD models from concept stage
- **Digital twin integration** expanding BIM use from design-through-operations lifecycle
- **AI in BIM** — automated clash detection, generative design, quantity extraction
- **Cloud collaboration** — Autodesk BIM 360/ACC, Trimble Connect, Bentley iTwin
- **Scan-to-BIM** growth from existing building renovation and infrastructure projects
- **IPD (Integrated Project Delivery)** models favoring early BIM engagement
