# Go-to-Market — BIM Global Outreach (iNCOMPLEX)

## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1–2)
**Goal:** Build outreach infrastructure and initial lead list

| Activity | Channels | Success Metric |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| LinkedIn Profiles Optimization | LinkedIn | 10+ inbound inquiries/month |
| CRM Setup (recommended: HubSpot/HubSpot CRM) | Internal | 500+ qualified leads in DB |
| Lead List Generation | LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Clutch, Construction directories | 200+ prospect companies |
| Outreach Script Development | Internal | 3 variants (US, UK, UAE) |
| Portfolio/Website Refresh | Web | BIM case studies, LOD examples, testimonials |
| Upwork Enterprise Profile | Upwork | 3+ Enterprise invitations/week |

**Target:** 20–500 employee firms in Tier 1 markets, recently posting BIM/Revit jobs

## Phase 2: Outbound Pilot (Months 2–4)
**Goal:** 50 outreaches/week with consultative messaging

| Activity | Channels | Success Metric |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| LinkedIn Connection + InMail | LinkedIn | 20% acceptance rate, 5% response rate |
| Email Drip Campaign | Email (Lemlist/Mailshake) | 30% open rate, 5% reply rate |
| Clutch Outreach | Clutch | 10+ project inquiries/month |
| Referral Program | Existing network | 3+ referrals/month |

**Messaging Framework (Pain-Focused):**
- "We help overloaded architecture and construction teams handle BIM production and MEP coordination during high-volume project phases."
- Avoid: "We provide BIM services."
- Lead with: staffing pressure → coordination problems → documentation overload

## Phase 3: Scaling (Months 4–6)
**Goal:** 200 outreaches/week, consistent pipeline

| Activity | Channels | Success Metric |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| LinkedIn Automation (Dux-Soup/LinkedHelper) | LinkedIn | 50+ conversations/month |
| Email at Scale | Email | 20+ qualified meetings/month |
| BIM Forums & Communities | Reddit (r/Revit, r/BIM), BIM 360 Community | 5+ inbound leads/month |
| Webinars/Content Marketing | LinkedIn Live, YouTube | 100+ views per asset |

**Key Indicators It's Working:**
- Repeat clients from Phase 2
- Inbound referrals from past conversations
- Consistent 5+ proposals submitted weekly
- Average deal size increasing with consultative positioning

## Phase 4: Optimization (Months 6+)
**Goal:** Predictable revenue, 70%+ from repeat/referral

- Refine ICP based on highest-converting segments
- Build case studies from successful engagements
- Automate lead scoring in CRM
- Expand to Tier 2 markets
