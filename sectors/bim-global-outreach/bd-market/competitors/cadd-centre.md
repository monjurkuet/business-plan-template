## Strengths
1. **Massive network** — 120+ franchise centers across India, 1,000+ trained engineers available
2. **Dual revenue model** — Both BIM services and training create talent pipeline and brand awareness
3. **Cost leadership** — Among the lowest-cost BIM/CAD outsourcing options in the market
4. **Geographic reach** — Centers in tier-2/3 Indian cities with lower labor costs
5. **Government ties** — Worked on many Indian government infrastructure projects

## Weaknesses
1. **Variable quality** — Franchise model leads to inconsistent quality across centers
2. **Process fragmentation** — Less centralized QA/QC compared to dedicated single-location firms
3. **Training-first brand** — Perception as training institute undermines services credibility
4. **Low-end positioning** — Typically gets lowest-complexity work, struggles with high-end BIM
5. **Management layers** — Franchise network adds coordination overhead for large projects

## Pricing Signals
- Est. $10–18/hr for BIM modeling (very competitive)
- Bulk discounts available
- Some of the lowest market rates
