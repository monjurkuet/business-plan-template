## Strengths
1. **MEP BIM depth** — Core strength in MEP coordination, mechanical/electrical/plumbing detailed modeling
2. **BIM consulting** — Offers strategic BIM implementation and standards development, not just production
3. **International project experience** — Delivered projects in UK (BIM Level 2), Middle East, and US
4. **Quality focus** — Strong QA processes, Navisworks clash detection, model auditing
5. **Mid-size agility** — Larger than boutique shops but smaller/faster than 1,000+ person firms

## Weaknesses
1. **Mumbai concentration** — Single delivery location creates risk and capacity ceiling
2. **Brand overlap** — Multiple names (BIM Elite, Hitech BIM) creates market confusion
3. **Modest scale** — 100–200 staff limits large enterprise contracts
4. **Marketing visibility** — Limited global brand presence, mostly referral-driven
5. **Narrow MEP focus** — Less capable for full architectural/structural BIM scope
