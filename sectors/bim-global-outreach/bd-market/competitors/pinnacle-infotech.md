## Strengths
1. **Scale** — 1,500+ employees, 2,600+ clients across 47 countries
2. **Longevity** — 30+ years in BIM outsourcijg, proven track record
3. **Full stack** — Architectural, structural, MEP, scan-to-BIM, BIM consulting, digital twin
4. **Global presence** — Offices in India, UK, USA, UAE, Germany, Singapore
5. **Brand recognition** — Well-known in AEC outsourcing space

## Weaknesses
1. **Premium pricing** — More expensive than generic Indian BIM providers
2. **Minimum engagement size** — Typically takes larger projects, less flexible for small firms
3. **Standardized processes** — Less flexible for custom workflows
4. **Sales-driven** — Transactional sales approach, less consultative
5. **Overhead** — Large org structure means slower responsiveness for small clients
