## ERRC Actions (Blue Ocean)

**Eliminate:**
- Transactional "cheap BIM labor" positioning
- Cold outreach without pain-point targeting
- One-size-fits-all pitches

**Reduce:**
- Generic "we provide BIM services" messaging
- Focus on hourly rates (shift to value-based)
- Broad untargeted email blasts

**Raise:**
- Architecture + engineering understanding (differentiator)
- Consultative outreach (solve problems, don't sell services)
- Quality standards (LOD control, clash-free deliverables)
- English communication and Western building code familiarity

**Create:**
- "BIM Production Partner" category (not vendor)
- Pain-focused outreach scripts (staffing pressure, coordination overload)
- Extended team positioning (they don't hire, they scale with us)
- Specialized MEP coordination support for overloaded firms
