# Pricing Guide — BIM Global Outreach (iNCOMPLEX)

## Rate Positioning Strategy

Position as **BIM Production Partner** (not commodity BIM labor). Target $50–$150/hr range for differentiated architecture + MEP expertise.

## Benchmark Rates (2025–2026)

| Service Type | Low-End (India/PH) | Mid-Market (Eastern Europe) | Premium (iNCOMPLEX target) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Architectural BIM Modeling | $15–25/hr | $30–50/hr | $50–80/hr |
| MEP Coordination & Clash Detection | $20–30/hr | $40–60/hr | $65–100/hr |
| Scan-to-BIM | $20–35/hr | $45–65/hr | $70–110/hr |
| BIM Visualization (Enscape/Lumion) | $25–40/hr | $50–70/hr | $75–120/hr |
| BIM Management/Consulting | $30–50/hr | $60–90/hr | $100–150/hr |
| Revit Family Creation | $15–25/hr | $30–45/hr | $50–70/hr |

## Competitive Pricing Insights

- US/UK/EU firms expect to pay $75–150/hr for quality BIM support
- Indian firms dominate at $15–30/hr but quality varies significantly
- Philippine firms: $18–35/hr, improving quality
- Eastern European: $30–60/hr, strong quality and English
- iNCOMPLEX sweet spot: **$60–100/hr** — competitive with Eastern Europe, positioned as architecture-MEP specialist

## Pricing Model Options

| Model | Best For | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Hourly | Short-term projects, initial relationships | Simple, low friction |
| Project-based (fixed fee) | Well-scoped deliverables, repeat work | Clients prefer predictability |
| Retainer (monthly block) | Ongoing production support | Best for long-term relationships, predictable revenue |
| Value-based | Large/complex projects | Higher margin but harder to sell initially |
