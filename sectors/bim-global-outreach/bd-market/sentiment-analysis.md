# Sentiment Analysis — BIM Global Outreach (iNCOMPLEX)

## Industry Sentiment Overview

| Segment | Sentiment | Key Drivers |
|---------|:---:|------------|
| US Architecture Firms | 🟡 Cautious but open | Staffing shortages, tight project margins, quality concerns about offshore |
| UK Architecture Firms | 🟢 Receptive | Strong BIM mandate, experienced with outsourcing, value quality |
| UAE Design-Build Firms | 🟢 Very receptive | High project volume, fast-track schedules, accustomed to international teams |
| Australian Construction | 🟡 Cautious | Growing BIM adoption, prefer local but flexible on support |

| MEP Consultants (Global) | 🟢 Receptive | Specialized MEP talent shortage, high coordination demands |

## Key Pain Points (Outreach Angle)

1. **Staffing pressure** — "We can't hire enough BIM/Revit staff fast enough"
2. **Coordination problems** — "MEP clashes are killing our project timelines"
3. **Documentation overload** — "We have 3 projects due and no bandwidth"
4. **Fast-track deadlines** — "We need LOD 400 models yesterday"
5. **Fixed fee pressure** — "Our margins are squeezed, but we can't sacrifice quality"
