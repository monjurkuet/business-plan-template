# Regulatory Requirements — Travel & Tourism in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration (if annual turnover > BDT 3 million)

### Sector-Specific Requirements
- Tourism operator license from Ministry of Civil Aviation and Tourism
- IATA accreditation for air ticketing agents
- Travel agency registration with Bangladesh Tourism Board
- Insurance requirements for tour operators
- Visa processing compliance (if providing visa services)

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Ministry of Civil Aviation and Tourism
- Bangladesh Tourism Board
- Bangladesh Parjatan Corporation (BPC)
- Civil Aviation Authority of Bangladesh (CAAB)
- IATA (for ticketing)
