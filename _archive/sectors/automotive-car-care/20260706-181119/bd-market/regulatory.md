# Regulatory Requirements — Automotive & Car Care in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration (if annual turnover > BDT 3 million)

### Sector-Specific Requirements
- Environmental clearance for chemical-based detailing (if applicable)
- Fire safety certificate for workshops
- BSTI compliance for imported automotive chemicals

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Bangladesh Road Transport Authority (BRTA)
- Department of Environment (DoE)
- Bangladesh Standards and Testing Institution (BSTI)
