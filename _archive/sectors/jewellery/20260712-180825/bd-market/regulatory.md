# Regulatory Requirements — Jewellery in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration (mandatory for jewellers)

### Sector-Specific Requirements
- BJS (Bangladesh Jewellers Samity) membership recommended
- Hallmarking compliance for gold jewellery
- VAT on gold: 5% (reduced from previous rates)
- Import duty on gold and precious stones
- AML compliance for high-value transactions

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Bangladesh Jewellers Samity (BJS)
- National Board of Revenue (NBR)
- Bangladesh Standards and Testing Institution (BSTI)
- Bangladesh Bank (AML reporting)
