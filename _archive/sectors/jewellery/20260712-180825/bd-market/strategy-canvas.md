## ERRC Grid

### Eliminate
- Physical showroom requirement (start online-only)
- Heavy gold inventory (work with suppliers on consignment)

### Reduce
- Bridal focus (let established players fight for this)
- Heritage/trust claims (we're new — don't fake it)

### Raise
- Trend responsiveness (new designs weekly, not seasonally)
- Online shopping experience (3D try-on, video previews, easy returns)
- Social media content quality (jewellery is visual — own this)

### Create
- Jewellery rental for events (no organized player)
- "Try at home" service (ship 3 options, keep 1, return 2)
- Gift curation service (pre-packaged gift boxes for occasions)
