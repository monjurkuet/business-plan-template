## ERRC Grid

### Eliminate
- TVC/traditional production (expensive, declining demand)
- Jack-of-all-trades positioning (be specialist first)

### Reduce
- Client count (focus on 10-15 high-value clients, not 50+ SMEs)
- Fixed retainer model (move to performance-based)

### Raise
- Short-form content output volume (daily, not weekly)
- AI tool adoption (cut costs, increase speed)
- Performance accountability (show ROI, not vanity metrics)

### Create
- Revenue-share pricing model (% of attributed sales)
- Influencer-as-a-service (curated network, matched to brand)
- Content-as-a-subscription (fixed monthly fee, guaranteed output)
