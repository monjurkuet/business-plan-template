# Regulatory Requirements — Media, Marketing & Digital in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration (if annual turnover > BDT 3 million)

### Sector-Specific Requirements
- BTRC compliance for digital advertising platforms
- Data privacy compliance (Digital Security Act 2023)
- Copyright registration for content
- Foreign advertising permits (for international campaigns)
- E-commerce compliance if selling digital products

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC)
- Ministry of Information and Broadcasting
- Copyright Office, Bangladesh
- Bangladesh ICT Division
