# Regulatory Requirements — Clothing & Fashion in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration (if annual turnover > BDT 3 million)

### Sector-Specific Requirements
- BSTI compliance for textile products
- Labeling requirements per Bangladesh Standards
- Import license for foreign brands/garments
- Factory compliance (if manufacturing): fire safety, labor law

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Ministry of Textiles and Jute
- Bangladesh Garment Manufacturers and Exporters Association (BGMEA)
- Bangladesh Standards and Testing Institution (BSTI)
- Department of Inspections for Factories and Establishments (DIFE)
