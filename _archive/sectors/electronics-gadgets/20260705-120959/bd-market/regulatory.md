# Regulatory Requirements — Electronics & Gadgets in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration (if annual turnover > BDT 3 million)

### Sector-Specific Requirements
- BSTI certification for electronics
- BTRC type approval for telecom devices
- Import registration with Chief Controller of Imports & Exports
- Energy labeling requirements for appliances
- E-waste management compliance (upcoming)

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Bangladesh Standards and Testing Institution (BSTI)
- Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC)
- Ministry of Commerce
- Department of Environment (DoE) — e-waste
