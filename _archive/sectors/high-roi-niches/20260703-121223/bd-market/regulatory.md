# Regulatory Requirements — High ROI Niches in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration (if annual turnover > BDT 3 million)

### Subsegment-Specific Requirements
- **Pet Care**: Veterinary license from Directorate of Livestock Services; animal health certification
- **Home Bakery**: Food safety license from Bangladesh Food Safety Authority; BSTI compliance
- **Creator Economy**: BTRC compliance for digital content; copyright registration
- **Fitness**: Sports ministry affiliation; fire safety for gym premises

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Bangladesh Food Safety Authority (BFSA)
- Directorate of Livestock Services
- Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC)
- Ministry of Youth and Sports
