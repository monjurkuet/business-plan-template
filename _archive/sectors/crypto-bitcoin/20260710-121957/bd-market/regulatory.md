# Regulatory Requirements — Crypto & Bitcoin in Bangladesh
## Regulatory Requirements

### Legal Status
**Crypto trading is LEGAL in Bangladesh.** This is a confirmed, non-negotiable fact.

### Business Registration
- Trade license from City Corporation/Upazila Parishad
- TIN certificate from NBR
- VAT registration

### Sector-Specific Requirements
- Bangladesh Bank guidelines on digital financial services
- BTRC compliance for digital platforms
- AML/KYC requirements per Bangladesh Bank circulars
- Foreign exchange regulations for cross-border crypto transactions

### Key Regulatory Bodies
- Bangladesh Bank (central bank)
- Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC)
- National Board of Revenue (NBR)
- Bangladesh Investment Development Authority (BIDA)
