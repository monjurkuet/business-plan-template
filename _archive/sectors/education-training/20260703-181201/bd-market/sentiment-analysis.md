## Voice of Customer (Common Complaints)

1. **"Batch size 50+ — teacher bujhay na properly"** — Overcrowded classrooms reducing learning quality
2. **"Fees baray, quality kome"** — Fee increases without commensurate quality improvement
3. **"Coaching center just crash kursi"** — Over-reliance on last-minute exam prep vs conceptual learning
4. **"Study abroad consultant taka niye palay gese"** — Consultant fraud in study abroad segment
5. **"Online class e kintu kichu shikhina"** — Online classes perceived as less effective than offline
6. **"English medium teacher qualified na"** — Teacher qualification concerns in some private institutions
