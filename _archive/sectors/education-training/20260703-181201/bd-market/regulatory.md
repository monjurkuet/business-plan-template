## Coaching Center Requirements (2018 Act)

- Registered with District Education Office
- Fee ceiling (cannot increase >50% annually)
- Class size limited to 30 students per batch
- Teacher qualification notification (minimum Bachelor's for HSC level)
- Building safety certificate (per RAJUK/fire service standards)
